About

John F. Kennedy once said, "Leadership and learning are indispensable to each other." As a high schooler, I embody this timeless wisdom, constantly exploring new horizons and embracing challenges with unwavering dedication. In the spirit of adventure and appreciation for the natural world, I, an avid hiker, photographer, and lover of our nation's parks, welcome you to my portfolio. As a high school student, I have found immense joy in exploring the great outdoors and capturing its breathtaking beauty through the lens of my camera. My passions extend beyond photography, as I am deeply committed to the environment and the art of leadership. Guided by a lineage of civil servants, I embrace challenges as opportunities to apply my unwavering determination and problem-solving skills. With a strong devotion to environmentalism and justice, I strive to be an advocate for our planet's well-being. As I navigate the demanding terrain of academic coursework and extracurricular endeavors, I am driven to make a meaningful impact upon others. Future aspirations lead me towards studying Wildlife Ecology, Economics, and Political Science, igniting my pursuit of a career dedicated to safeguarding our environment. I invite you to explore my work and connect with me at s1705256@online.houstonisd.org.
